About Me

Hello! I'm Rhiannan, a third year Computer Science major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am pursuing a concentration in Artificial Intelligence and People. I have mostly back-end development experience, but will be working with front-end and back-end this year to become a well-rounded full-stack developer. This is my first time building a website from scratch and I know I have a lot to learn, but I look forward to learning!
I was first introduced to computer science in high school when I took Intro to Programming and AP Computer Science my final years, where I found an interest in computer programming. Throughout my college experience, I have become increasingly more passionate about touching the lives of others through software.
I would describe myself as intellectually curious and always seeking new heights. I enjoy creating and contributing to things and solving problems. I was born in Puerto Rico and lived there for thirteen years until I moved to Maryland, where I currently reside. Some of my hobbies include painting, listening to music, and playing video games. It is a dream of mine to be able to build my own PC gaming system by the end of this year to be able to enjoy playing video games some more.
